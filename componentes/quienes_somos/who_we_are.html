OUR SCHOOL

The Caminos del Saber school, is a private educational establishment, which offers educational service in a single day, of a mixed nature, legally recognized by the authorities competent, and that serves formal education at the Preschool levels (Pre- garden, Garden and Transition) and basic primary, in calendar A. The Caminos del Saber school is an educational entity that is recognized by the Colombian state with approval resolution No. 3131-6 of May 2013 and the confirmation of the license according to resolution No. 2294-6 of July 24, 2020, and its respective modification No 2607-6 of August 26, 2020 Emanated by the Secretary of education of the department of Caldas.

Mission

The Caminos del Saber school is an educational community of excellence that encourages its students to build their knowledge day by day, promoting search for truth and the exercise of responsible freedom. We also generate environments conducive to an adequate climate of school coexistence.

Vision

The Caminos del Saber school will be recognized as an institution with high quality educational, based on values and English and a deep ethical, humanistic and obtaining students with a profile of academic, human and Christian quality for optimal performance in social, diverse and competitive spaces.

Our values

  • To build an order where we can all be happy: DEMOCRACY

  • To discover and interact with the world: KNOWLEDGE

  • To weave social networks: COMMUNICATION

  • To live in harmony: COEXISTENCE

  • To learn to manage freedom: AUTONOMY

  • To guarantee the survival of the planet and the human species: TAKE CARE OF YOURSELF SELF AND THE ENVIRONMENT.
